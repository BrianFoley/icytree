IcyTree

This browser-based phylogenetic tree viewer is based on the JavaScript library phylo.js. It aims to be a (very) lightweight alternative to FigTree that is useful in situations where rapid visualisation of tree log files such as those produced by BEAST 2 or MASTER is more important than the generation of publication-quality graphics.

IcyTree is compatible with current GNU/Linux and OS X versions of Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome. Your mileage may vary with other browser/OS combinations.
